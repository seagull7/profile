Merkuri

Description

  • Me, Travis, Hussein

  • merkuri.space/login

  • September, 2018

This project is to help optimize project management on a small scale with a budget analysis provided. MERKURI is a play on Mercury which is the Roman God of wealth and prosperity. An admin would be allowed to add members to his project with either a phone number or email, and assign tasks based on a schedule, description, and budget.
