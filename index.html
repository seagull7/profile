HAPPY ANIVERARY!

Dear Kaitlyn,
It’s strange how three years can both feel like a lifetime and like only three minutes. It seems we haven’t had one year without massive change huh? Being back in Houston, going to school, working with the Ronster, getting staffed everywhere but Houston and getting our own apartment again. Its been a crazy time. I don’t know quite how you put up with some of the ridiculousness I bring to the table, but I’m glad we can be back here with your family again now. They have been such a blessing to me as well. It’s hard for me to write this note with how negative my mind has been about things and how negative it was this whole past year. I’ve honestly had a hard time with the concept of “celebrating” our marriage with how poor of a husband I feel I have been. But, I’m so thankful for you and for all you have brought into my life. I would literally not be here without you and all of this would have been impossible without having you at my back. I love the team that you and I are, and I have loved being able to spend time with you in our new apartment playing games and getting to do crazy projects. I love you and am excited to spend this next year continuing to figure out how to do life with you. It’s only a one page flat site, but I made this site to bring you back to a few things from three years ago. I hope you enjoy it. Happy anniversary.
Love,
Sam
